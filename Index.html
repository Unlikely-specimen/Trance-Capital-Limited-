Our Story

Trance Capital Limited is a Lusaka-based, PACRA-registered money lending company founded in 2024. It offers short- to medium-term, payroll-backed loans at below-market rates to individuals and employees of partner organizations, aiming to reduce financial stress and promote stability. The company is fully compliant with Zambian financial regulations. To strengthen its impact, Trance Capital focuses on: - Expanding partnerships with financially constrained but established organizations - Enhancing operational efficiency in loan disbursement and repayment - Ensuring regulatory compliance to mitigate legal risks - Improving accessibility through user-friendly loan platforms - Customizing loan products to meet diverse financial needs - Building trust via transparent communication with stakeholders

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide tailored financial solutions that empower individuals and assist organizations to deliver financial solutions to their employees. We strive to deliver accessible, transparent, and innovative financial services.

Our Vision

To serve individuals and organizations and assist them access affordable capital to finance their dreams. We envisage expanding to provide tailored services to our clientele with their unique needs.
